About me!

Hey! I'm Dani. Here's my brief background and fun facts about me: I was adopted from Hunan Province, China, into a loving family. My favorite food is steak frites from Burdicks! And a few fears of mine are heights and spiders. Now we can get into the present! I am a freshman studying computer and electrical engineering at Worcester Polytechnic Institute. I previously graduated from Bellows Falls Union High School and enrolled in a pre-college program for my senior year at Vermont State University (VTSU). At VTSU, I really got a preview of college life by living in a dorm room, eating in the dining hall, and walking to classes! I met many great people and professors there, which ultimately made me excited about college the upcoming year. I also love to draw, play a variety of games, and spend time with my family!
